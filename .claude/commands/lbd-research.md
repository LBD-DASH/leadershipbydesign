Research a company before outreach or a call.

Usage: /lbd-research [company name]

1. Search the web for the company + South Africa
2. Find: what they do, approximate size, industry, key leadership/HR people, any recent news
3. Check if they're in an excluded industry (banks, FSI, insurance, education, consulting) — if so, flag immediately
4. Check if company size is under 500
5. Suggest a personalised opening line Kevin could use in an email or call
6. Identify the likely pain point based on their industry and size

Output a brief dossier Kevin can glance at before a call or use for manual outreach.
